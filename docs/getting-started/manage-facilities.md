# Manage Facilities

A **Facility** represents a physical location, branch, or operational site within your organization that uses AuditMate.  
Each facility may have its own users, machine types, and machines associated with it, allowing AuditMate to organize and report data accurately across different locations.


![Manage Facilities](./images/manage-facilities.png "width=800 nozoom")    
**Access:** Top Menu → **Admin**, Left Menu → **Facilities**  

## Add a Facility

<div class="doc-flex-row">
<div>

You can create a new facility to organize and manage operations within AuditMate.  <br><br>1. Click **Create** to open the **Create Facility** form.  <br> 2. Fill in required details. <br> 3. Click **Save** to add the facility to the system. <br><br> Once saved, the new facility will appear in the **Facilities** list.

</div>
<div>

![Create Facility Form](./images/create-facility-form.png "width=400")

</div>
</div>


## Edit or Update a Facility

<div class="doc-flex-row">
<div>

To modify an existing facility’s details: <br><br>1. Locate the facility in the **Facilities** list. <br> 2. Click on the corresponding **Edit icon** of the facility you want to update. <br> 3. Update any necessary details. <br> 4. Click **Save** to apply changes. <br><br> Once saved, the updated facility information will reflect immediately in the **Facilities** list.

</div>
</div>

---

## Delete a Facility

<div class="doc-flex-row">
<div>

You can remove a facility from the system when it is no longer needed. <br><br>1. Click on the corresponding **Delete icon** of the facility you want to delete. <br> 2. Confirm the deletion when prompted. <br><br> ⚠️ **Note:** Deleting a facility is permanent and cannot be undone. Ensure that no users or records are actively linked to the facility before proceeding.

</div>
</div>
