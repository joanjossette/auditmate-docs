# Parts & Operations

Parts and Operations form the core structure of AuditMate MFG Revision Manager. Together, they define how CNC programs are organized, reviewed, approved, and executed throughout their lifecycle.

This structure ensures that:

<div style="margin-left: 1.5rem">

- CNC programs are grouped logically
- Revisions are tracked and auditable
- Only authorized programs reach production machines
- Changes are traceable at every stage

</div>

A **Part** represents the job or item being manufactured, while **Operations** represent the individual machining steps performed on that Part.
