# Operations

An **Operation** represents a specific machining step performed on a Part. Each Operation is associated with at least one CNC program and defines how that program is executed on a machine.

![Parts Page](./images/operations-page.png "width=800 nozoom")  
**Access:** Top Menu → **Parts and Operations**, select a part from the list, then go to **Operations** via the Left Menu or Center Menu. The Left Panel shows all operations for the selected part.

## Purpose of Operations

Operations allow you to:
- Manage CNC programs at a detailed level
- Assign programs to specific machines
- Track revisions and approvals per machining step
- Control execution in production environments

This level of control ensures accuracy, accountability, and traceability for every machining process.

## Operations in the Workflow

Each Operation progresses through the workflow independently while remaining linked to its parent Part. Changes, approvals, and revisions are recorded at the Operation level, creating a complete audit trail for every CNC program.
